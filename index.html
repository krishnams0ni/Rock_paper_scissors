Rock paper scissors, believed to have originated in Japan as "jan-ken-pon," is a classic game enjoyed worldwide. It's played for fun, as a tiebreaker or even as a decision-making tool. With many variations like "rock paper scissors lizard Spock," players must make strategic choices based on their opponent's past moves and body language, making it a fun and challenging game of strategy. Interestingly, it's also been studied for its applications in decision-making and conflict resolution. Rock paper scissors is a beloved game that has stood the test of time, providing entertainment and fair play to people worldwide.
